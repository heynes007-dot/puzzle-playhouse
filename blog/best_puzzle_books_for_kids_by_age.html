🧒 Ages 4–6: Beginner Puzzle Books

At this stage, children are just starting to recognise letters and simple words. Puzzle books should be fun, colourful and easy to complete.

  • Simple word searches (large grids)
  • Picture-based puzzles
  • Easy matching and spotting activities

Benefits: Builds confidence, improves letter recognition, and introduces problem-solving in a fun way.

🧑 Ages 7–9: Developing Skills

Children in this age group can handle more structured challenges and begin to enjoy a wider variety of puzzles.

  • Word searches with longer words
  • Simple crosswords
  • Word scrambles

Benefits: Improves spelling, vocabulary, and concentration while keeping learning enjoyable.
